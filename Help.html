How can we help you?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to the Settings page and look for the "Security" section. You can update your password there. If you cannot log in, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

Where can I view my grades?

Navigate to the "Grades" tab in the sidebar. You will see a breakdown of your midterm and final grades for all enrolled subjects.

How do I contact my professor?

In the "Courses" page, click on a specific subject card. You will find the professor's contact information and consultation hours listed there.

Can I change the website theme?

Yes! You can toggle between Light and Dark mode using the sun/moon icons in the top right header, or by visiting the Settings page.
